I am
Tim Berners-Lee

Hi

In 2001 I became a Fellow of the Royal Society and in 2004 I was knighted.

In 1994 I founded the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) at the Laboratory of Computer Science (LCS) at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in Boston. It was made up of companies that wanted to work together to create Web standards and also improve the Web. It was quickly agreed that the technology should be free of royalties and patents. I have served as director of this W3C since it was founded.
